Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get asked the most.

Is ASCEND only for Nigerian businesses?

While we are optimized for the Nigerian regulatory and financial environment (e.g., CAC, SMEDAN), the core business tools (CRM, Marketing, E-commerce) are universally beneficial for any SME operating in Africa.

Do I need technical knowledge to use the platform?

Absolutely not. ASCEND is built for ease of use. If you can use a smartphone, you can run your business on ASCEND. We handle all the complexity so you can focus on growth.

How does the 'Access to Finance' pillar work?

We analyze your business data to calculate your **Ascending Score** which acts as a pre-qualification metric. This score is shared with our network of approved lenders and grant providers, ensuring you only apply for funding you are eligible for, dramatically increasing your success rate.
